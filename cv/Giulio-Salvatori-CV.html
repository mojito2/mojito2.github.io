Giulio Salvatori

Theoretical physicist — scattering amplitudes & positive geometry

I look for the hidden geometry behind how particles scatter, reformulating quantum field theory in the language of curves on surfaces, polytopes, and tropical geometry. Currently an MSCA Global Fellow between the Institute for Advanced Study and the Max Planck Institute for Physics.
